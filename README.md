# 🛒 Superstore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents a comprehensive Sales Performance Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions including revenue, profit, product categories, regions, and customer segments. The dashboard is designed to provide executive-level insights and enable data-driven decision-making for sales strategy and operational efficiency.

# 🔍 Overview

The dashboard delivers a holistic view of business performance, covering key KPIs such as total sales, profit, quantity sold, and average delivery metrics. It integrates multiple analytical perspectives including category performance, shipping efficiency, regional analysis, and time-based trends.
The design emphasizes clarity, consistency, and usability, ensuring stakeholders can quickly interpret data and derive actionable insights.

# 📊 Key Insights & Analysis
- Sales Distribution by Category: Office Supplies leads overall revenue generation, followed by Technology and Furniture, indicating strong demand in operational products.
- Shipping Mode Analysis: Standard Class dominates sales contribution, suggesting cost-efficiency preference among customers, while Same Day delivery shows minimal usage.
- Sub-Category Performance: Products like Phones, Chairs, and Binders drive the majority of sales, highlighting key revenue-generating SKUs.
- Customer Segment Analysis: Consumer segment contributes the largest share (48%), followed by Corporate and Home Office, enabling targeted marketing strategies.
- Regional Insights: Interactive region filter (Central, East, West, South) allows comparative analysis across geographical markets.
- Time Series Trends:
      - Monthly Sales (YoY): Demonstrates growth trends and seasonal demand fluctuations between 2019 and 2020.
      - Monthly Profit (YoY): Highlights profitability variations and identifies months with high/low margins.
- Geographical Analysis: Map visualization shows sales and profit distribution across states, enabling location-based decision-making.

# 🛠️ Technical Implementation
- Built a robust data model integrating sales, customer, and product datasets
- Developed optimized DAX measures for KPIs including Sales, Profit, Quantity, and derived metrics
- Implemented interactive slicers (Region filter) to enhance user-driven exploration
- Designed visuals using:
    - Bar charts for category and sub-category analysis
    - Line/area charts for time-series trends
    - Donut chart for segment distribution
    - Map visualization for geographic insights
- Applied UI/UX best practices with a consistent theme, structured layout, and clear visual hierarchy
